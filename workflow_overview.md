# Workflow Overview

## Input
External articles, company announcements, competitor launches, and market developments.

## Process
1. Gather source links or notes
2. Extract key information
3. Use AI to summarize the content
4. Group findings into themes
5. Highlight implications for business decision-making

## Output
A short weekly or monthly digest that shows:
- what changed
- why it matters
- what teams should pay attention to
